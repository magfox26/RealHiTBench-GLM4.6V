Data Dictionary - HCP Influenza Vaccination by Hospital and County
Field Format Definition
Influenza_Season Plain Text October 01, 2023 through March 31, 2024
State Plain Text California
CDPH_HAI_HCP_FLU_VACCINATION Plain Text California Department of Public Health, Healthcare-Associated Infections Program, Health Care Personnel Influenza Vaccination
County Plain Text Reported County
Facility_ID Plain Text Facility_ID is the primary key used to track facility information for all facilities stored in the Electronic Licensing Management System (ELMS) for CDPH Center for Health Care Quality, Licensing and Certification Program. 
Facility_Name Plain Text Facility name for facilities reporting to National Healthcare Safety Network (NHSN) healthcare personnel influenza vaccination data for the 2023-24 influenza season, specifically October 1, 2023, through March 31, 2024 for California General Acute Care Hospitals reporting.  
Hospital_Type Plain Text HAI Program designation for hospital types. These include major teaching, community (<125 beds, 125-250 beds, and >250 beds), pediatric, critical access, long-term acute care (LTAC), and free-standing rehabilitation hospitals.
HCP_Total_Number_Vaccinated Number Total number of Health Care Personnel (HCP) vaccinated. HCP include all paid (employee) and unpaid (licensed independent practitioners, other contract personnel and adult students/trainees and volunteers) persons working in health care settings and have the potential to transmit influenza to and from other HCP and patients. 
HCP_Total_Number_Working Number Total number of Health Care Personnel (HCP) working
HCP_Percent_Vaccinated Number Percentage of HCP vaccinated.
Emp_Number_Vaccinated Number Total number of hospital employees vaccinated
Emp_Number_Working Number Total number of hospital employees working
Emp_Percent_Vaccinated Number Percentage of hospital employees vaccinated
Lip_Number_Vaccinated Number Total number of licensed independent practitioners vaccinated
Lip_Number_Working Number Total number of licensed independent practitioners working
Lip_Percent_Vaccinated Number Percentage of licensed independent practitioners vaccinated
Cont_Number_Vaccinated Number Total number of other contract personnel vaccinated
Cont_Number_Working Number Total number of other contract personnel working
Cont_Percent_Vaccinated Number Percentage of contract personnel vaccinated
StudVol_Number_Vaccinated Number Total number of students, trainees, and volunteers vaccinated
StudVol_Number_Working Number Total number of students, trainees, and volunteers working
StudVol_Percent_Vaccinated Number Percentage of students, trainees, and volunteers vaccinated
Met 2020 goal of 90% (>=90% in 2023-24) Plain Text In 2023-24, hospitals should have achieved at least 90% (Healthy People 2020 goal) HCP vaccination coverage. Did hospital achieve at least 90% HCP vaccination coverage in 2023-24?  Yes or No.
Notes Plain Text “Non-reporter” indicates if hospital did not report data (all data fields are blank); "Reported combined data" indicates when hospital reports consolidated data with another hospital.
Additional information Inpatient rehabilitation units are excluded, for detailed explanation see Technical Notes.
Source Influenza Vaccination among Health Care Personnel in California Hospitals, 2022-23 Season. Healthcare-Associated Infections Program, California Department of Public Health.
