General notes
Hampshire FRS and Isle of Wight FRS merged on 31st March 2021. Figures for previous years are the sum of the figures for the two individual FRSs.
North West Fire Control is not an FRS, but employs control staff and should be included to be consistent. It was formed in 2014.
Staff on dual contracts have been recorded more than once e.g. firefighters on both a wholetime and an on-call contract are included in the wholetime firefighter and retained duty system firefighter figures above.
 
Detailed notes can be found in the guidance document, which has been extended this year.
"-" refers to data not captured.
Notes on data adjustments
A huge amount of work has been undertaken by FRSs to improve the quality of data, including in previous years, and Home Office are grateful for this. This explains some small amendments in the national and local figures in previous years.
Note on 2007 and 2008
Figures for fire authorities in 2007 and 2008 do not add to the England total.
