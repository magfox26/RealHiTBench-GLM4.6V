Due to a formula error, religion breakdowns were reported in the wrong order for 1 April 2012 Army Reserve and the RAF Reserves Other Ranks. All proportions were reported correctly and 30 numbers have been revised. The largest change is from 210 to 20 830, due to the Army Reserve Christian number being mis-reported as 210 which is the number relating to Unknown Religion. 
1. Christian includes personnel declaring the following on Joint Personnel Administration (JPA): Christian Tradition, Christian Scientist, Church of Jesus Christ Of Latter-Day Saints (Mormon), Jehovah's Witness, Unitarian and Other Christian Tradition.
2. Other Religions includes personnel declaring the following on Joint Personnel Administration (JPA): Druid, Pagan, Rastafarian, Spiritualist, Zoroastrian (Parsee), Wicca, Baha'i and Other Religions.
3. Unknown includes those with an unrecorded religion and those who choose not to declare.
4. Of the 35,540 personnel on strength as at 1 October 2016, 95.8% have a known religion.
